MARS

Mars is the next step in space exploration. Mars is the next step because it will make us a multi planetary species and give us a better understanding of what space exploration is going to look like. Mars is the fourth planet in our solar system and would take about seven months to get there from earth. No human has ever gone to Mars, only man made rovers have gone to Mars. These rovers have taken pictures and collected data of what Mars looks like and what kind of environment Mars holds. This poster above is meant to show and promote what Mars colonization is going to look like.
