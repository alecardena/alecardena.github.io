WHAT IS SPACE EXPLORATION?

What is Space exploration? Why is it important to humans? Space exploration is important because it can give us a better understanding of what is out there. It can also make us a multi planetary species which is important for humans in case something happens to Earth. Space exploration can open a number of different doors and improve lives in many different ways. Focusing on space exploration can bring new technologies and it can lead to things like improving human health, protecting our planet, and making many new different scientific discoveries.
