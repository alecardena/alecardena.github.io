The Solar System

This is our Solor System. Earth is our home planet and is where we will start from. Your mission is to try to get to Neptune. Once you click start you will see our planet Earth and you will pick which out of the two planets below is next to earth to get to Neptune. If you guess right you will move on to the next planet and do the same there until you get to Neptune. If you do not guess right you will have to start all over.
